What zero is

zero is a free, open-source desktop app that reads your Gmail on your own Mac, keeps the threads that still need you, and archives the rest reversibly. It runs on your machine using your own Google sign-in and your own AI provider. We don't host it or run it for you — you do.

What you need

  • A Mac that meets the requirements on the download page.
  • Your own Google account, which you connect through Google's sign-in.
  • Your own access to an AI assistant (Claude by default), which zero uses to judge threads and draft replies. Any cost or limit there is between you and that provider.

You're responsible for keeping your accounts and your machine secure, and for using zero only with accounts you're allowed to use.
